01 — Identity

02 — Color

Warm paper, ink, two signals

A warm, low-saturation base keeps long docs comfortable. Coral is the brand voice and primary action; teal is reserved for keywords, success, and matches inside output. Both share the same lightness and chroma — only the hue changes.

05 — Voice & tone

Friendly, plain, never precious

The name is a wink — the writing is straight. Short sentences. Verbs first. We help, we don't lecture.

Plain over clever

Say what it does. Save the jokes for the name and the 404 page.

Yours, not ours

It's your setup, your words. We never call your notes "content."

Lowercase calm

Match the prompt. Lowercase headlines, no exclamation pile-ups.

✓ We say

"Terminal help for your setup."

"Add a file, add a section — it just shows up."

"Forgot your own alias? halpme git."

✗ We don't

"The ultimate AI-powered knowledge platform."

"Revolutionize your developer experience!!"

"Leverage synergistic documentation."
